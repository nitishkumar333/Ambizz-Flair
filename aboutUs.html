About Us

AmbizzFlair is an eponymous luxury label that celebrates women through its classy and chic couture collections. Our label epitomizes elegance and style with a deep emphasis on craftsmanship and traditional styles fused with modern trend and comfort. The designs use hand embroidery with bold play with colors that stand out from the crowd. We enjoy the challenge and the process that goes into making each and every outfit. Every garment we create has it’s own story, from picking the fabric, vivid motifs, color has it’s own challenge and that’s make it unique. A dress must make, in an instant, a statement without the help of accessories. Whether it’s art, architecture, or an embroidery - a good design should have the power to move you. When the proportions and colors are right, it makes you feel a certain way. At AmbizzFlair, we’re forever chasing that happy feeling and we try to capture it and give it to our customers. Being from Varanasi also known as Banaras a small town in India it’s very important for me to keep experimenting with Indian textiles by incorporating my signature color, prints and embellishments differently each time. Each piece is glam.
